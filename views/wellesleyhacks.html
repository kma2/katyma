Wellesley Hacks, 2015-2016

WHACK is a hackathon held at Wellesley College each fall and spring semester. A hackathon focused on supporting women in tech, WHACK brings together students from schools throughout the greater Boston area for a weekend of ideation, hacking, and mentorship. I've been designing WHACK's branding and visual identity since fall 2015.
