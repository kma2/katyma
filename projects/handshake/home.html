Handshake Redesign

Redesigning a university talent platform



timeline

1 week

role

Interaction design, wireframing, user research

about

As a UX designer at the MIT Media Lab’s Civic Media group, I’ve had the opportunity to work on several projects related to the development of Media Cloud, an open-source platform for studying media ecosystems used by media researchers, academics, activists, and organizations around the world. I worked closely with researchers at Civic Media and the lead engineer of Media Cloud to improve the user experience for both novice and expert users.

Problem

Since Media Cloud was originally built for "expert" users (academic researchers at MIT and Harvard we work closely with), its challenging for novice users to understand the platform (who don't have access to a member of our team to help onboard them). Due to recent press, people all over the world have started using the platform to do their own media research. With a growing user base, it's mission critical that we design with novice users in mind.
